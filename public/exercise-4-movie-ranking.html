My Top 5 Movies of all-time.

Akame Ga Kill

This is my favourite anime. It is packed with actions!

Genocidal Organ

This is also my favourite anime movie. The movie is all about Military Techno-Thriller!

Dune

One of my favourite movie. This movie is also based on the RTS video game called Dune 1992. The Movie is all about the war for the resources called "spice" in planet Arrakis. It is a planet of desert but rich of spices and the main reason why the three opposing houses are eager to take it themselves that will cause a bloody war.

Dead Space

Also one of my favorite movie. It is also inspired from the video game series Dead Space 2008. It is a Sci-Fi Horror Film that tells about the lone Engineer who stranded in a mining ship in space, that he is forced to fight for the survival against the unknown horrors lurking in their ship.

Star Wars

One of my favourite classics! The movie is all about the colossal war between the twisted empire and the righteous rebels.
